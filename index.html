About KayBold

KayBold Consulting is led by Benita Kayembe, a Harvard and Cornell-trained advisor originally from the DRC's Katanga Province. She has contributed to reports for the World Economic Forum, published peer-reviewed research on just energy transitions, and developed case studies for the Harvard Ministerial Leadership Program.
